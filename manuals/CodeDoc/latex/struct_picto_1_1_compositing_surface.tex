\subsection{Detailed Description}
A base class that represents an area with a drawing on it. 

Q\-Images are converted and drawn onto \hyperlink{struct_picto_1_1_compositing_surface}{Compositing\-Surface} objects. Multiple \hyperlink{struct_picto_1_1_compositing_surface}{Compositing\-Surface} objects are in turn drawn onto a \hyperlink{class_picto_1_1_visual_target}{Visual\-Target} which is what is finally rendered on screen. In essence, a \hyperlink{struct_picto_1_1_compositing_surface}{Compositing\-Surface} can be thought of as an image object that is drawn in a language that the \hyperlink{class_picto_1_1_visual_target}{Visual\-Target} understands, or looked at another way, a compositing surface is a translater, translating from the language of the Q\-Image to the language of the \hyperlink{class_picto_1_1_visual_target}{Visual\-Target}. \begin{DoxyAuthor}{Author}
