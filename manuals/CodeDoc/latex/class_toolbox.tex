\subsection{Detailed Description}
A \hyperlink{class_toolbox}{Toolbox} widget containing groups of buttons that have some affect on activity in the designer. 

The \hyperlink{class_toolbox}{Toolbox} is essentially a collection of \hyperlink{class_tool_group}{Tool\-Group} objects, each of which appears in its own maximizable/minimizable sub-\/section of a widget. When we first created the \hyperlink{class_toolbox}{Toolbox}, we supported multiple types of \hyperlink{class_tool_group}{Tool\-Group} classes for creating \hyperlink{class_tool_group}{Tool\-Group} objects. These could still be easily supported, but right now we are only creating Tool\-Groups from the \hyperlink{class_asset_tool_group}{Asset\-Tool\-Group} since the other Tool\-Groups were either not particularly useful (ie. \hyperlink{class_background_tool_group}{Background\-Tool\-Group}) or no longer conceptually meaningful (ie. \hyperlink{class_property_tool_group}{Property\-Tool\-Group}).

Most of the more complicated work needed by the \hyperlink{class_toolbox}{Toolbox} is happening in the \hyperlink{class_tool_group}{Tool\-Group} and its sub-\/classes. The \hyperlink{class_toolbox}{Toolbox} itself pretty much just collects Tool\-Groups, and lays them out in a \hyperlink{class_toolbox}{Toolbox} framework. \begin{DoxyAuthor}{Author}
