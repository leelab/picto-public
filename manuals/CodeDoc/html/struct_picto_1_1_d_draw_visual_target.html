virtual void draw (QPoint location, QPoint compositingSurfaceOffset, QSharedPointer< CompositingSurface > compositingSurface)=0
 Draws the compositing surface onto the visual target. The compositingSurfaceOffset is the offset at which the compositing surface should be drawn from location to assure that the "zero" point of the compositing surface is at location. For example, a cursor compositing surface might request to be drawn at 0,0, however the center of the cursor might be at position 5,5 or 7,7 within the compositing surface depending on the size of the cursor. The offset tells the system, move the compositing surface up and to the left by 5,5 or 7,7 so that the center of the cursor is at 0,0. The returned CompositingSurface should set up with a QImage by the calling function.
